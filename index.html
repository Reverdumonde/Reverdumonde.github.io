About Us

We are a group of high school students passionate about fashion and sustainability. Our project is all about giving discarded clothes a new life by transforming them into stylish, one-of-a-kind pieces. Every item in our collection is handmade with creativity and care, proving that fashion can be both trendy and eco-friendly. Join us in making a difference
